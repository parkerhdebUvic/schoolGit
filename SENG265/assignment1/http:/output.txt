      <h1><font size="+1">Job Seekers</font></h1>
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
      <p>Margot Knight wrote on LinkedIn &quot;<em>Karae is a no-nonsense, incredibly 
        connected, and knowledgeable woman. She checked out my resume and gave 
        me advise on how to change it so my resume will get noticed and gave me 
        contacts to check out for job possibilities. What impressed me most was 
        her to the point but very personable style. If you are looking for work 
        make sure you contact Karae before you start!</em>&quot;</p>
      <p><br>
        Are you a job seeker? Answer the following questions yes or no.</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Job Seekers</strong> <font color="#804040"><strong>know</strong></font> 
          what type of work they want to do - they just have not been able to 
          find a job. Is this you?<br>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Job Seekers</strong> <font color="#804040"><strong>have</strong></font> 
          direction and ideas of what type of work to apply for. Is this you?<br>
        <li><strong>Job Seekers <font color="#804040">need </font></strong> polished 
          resumes to get the desired effect - interviews! Are you getting interviews?<br>
        
        <li><strong>Job Seekers <font color="#804040">need</font></strong><font color="#804040"> 
          <font color="#000000">interview skills</font></font> that will land 
          them the job. Are you getting job offers?
      </ul>
      If you answered yes to one of those questions click on the link below that applies to you.
	  <ul>
        <li><strong>Job Seekers</strong> <font color="#804040"><strong>need</strong></font> 
          <strong><font color="#804040">Marketing Materials</font></strong> <a href="resumes.htm">(resume, 
          cover letter, and references)</a> to sell their product (themselves) 
          and the services they offer<br>
        </li>
        <li><strong>Job Seekers</strong> <font color="#804040"><strong>need</strong></font> 
          <strong><font color="#804040">Search Strategies</font></strong> <a href="interviews.htm">(information 
          interviews, interview answers, and networking skills)</a> to find companies 
          that may employ them</li>
        <p>&nbsp;</p>
      </ul>
      <p align="center"><font size="+1">Why would you, a Job Seeker, want to hire 
        </font></p>
      <p align="center"><font size="+1">a Morgan Ebbett Career Developer?</font></p>
      <p align="center"><font size="+1"> Here are 10 reasons <a href="why hire.htm"><font size="+1">why to hire a Career Developer
        </font></a><font size="+2">.</font></font></p>
