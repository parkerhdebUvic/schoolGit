WHAT IS HINF? BLOG ABOUT PARKER

About
Parker

What brought you into the program?

When I first started at UVic, I had only a vague idea of the direction I wanted to go with my studies! I knew I wanted work populations and technology, but it wasn't until my second year that I even heard of Health Informatics! HINF offered me the perfect blend of purpose, skillset, and income that I was looking for.


Did you have any prior experience?

Only a little! I was fortunate to spend a summer with the INSPIRE program at UVic where our team designed software for emergency homeless shelters; it was an absolutely invaluable experience.


What is your current academic background?

I'm in my 3rd year of the combined Computer Science & Health Informatics program, so most of my courses have focused on math, statistics, programming, computer science, and Canadian health policy!


What would you say are the top 3 most important skills for a health informatitian?

I can only speak from what I've picked up through my classes and heard from graduates working in the industry! Health Informatics is an incredibly broad field, so the top 3 skills for the direction I'm going includes the following:

  1. Communication & Empathy
  2. Statistics & Research
  3. Programming & Design
